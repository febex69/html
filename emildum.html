What Happens When You Cum

Ejaculation is the release of semen from the penis. It usually happens during sexual activity or masturbation. It involves both physical and mental processes.

Physical Effects

  • Muscle contractions
  • Increased heart rate
  • Short-term energy release
  • Refractory period (time before you can ejaculate again)

Mental Effects

  • Release of dopamine → feeling of reward
  • Release of prolactin → relaxation
  • Release of oxytocin → calmness or bonding
  • Temporary clarity or mental reset for some people

Notes

The effects vary from person to person. Some feel energized afterward, some feel tired. There’s no long-term cognitive boost; the clarity people feel is mostly due to tension release.
